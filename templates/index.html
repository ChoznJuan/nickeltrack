About the data

Nickel values from Mislankar & Zirwas (2013) Low-Nickel Diet Scoring System and the EFSA 2020 Scientific Opinion on Nickel in Food. Scoring: each food is 0–10 points per serving based on µg nickel (10 pts ≈ 91–100 µg; foods over 100 µg/serving are flagged "avoid"). Daily target is 15 points (≈150 µg).

⚠️ This is a reference tool, not medical advice. A low nickel diet should only be undertaken under supervision of a dermatologist or qualified medical doctor.
